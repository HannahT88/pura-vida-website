The Xmas Coders

The Xmas Coders is a team of two young ambitious entreprenuers determined to make a career for themselves within the christmas tech industry, an up and coming seasonal field. They first met at a guest lecture about the Palestinian conflict of which they cannot remember much, and since then have been on many adventures together.

This includes:

  • Surfing in cornwall
  • Serving mulled wine at York Christmas Market
  • Midnight essay writing library sessions
  • Eating too much chocolate
  • Learning alcoholic limits
  • Extreme oven cleaning at 3am

Andreea is a small but lively woman prone to wild emotions, which we have discovered can only be mediated by coffee or calling Hannah. When shes up her energy can light up the room and make everyone seem like her friend. When shes down its better just to stay away. Andreea speaks 5 languages: Romanian, English, Italian, French and Spanish, and has the most melodic accent in order to prevent you from guessing which is her mother tongue.

Hannah by contrast is a measured and mellow individual who prefers to experience drama from a distance rather than personally (hence the aquaintance with the above). Contrary to popular belief she is not a christmas elf but the grinch.
